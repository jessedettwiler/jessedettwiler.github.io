Bio

Swiss composer/producer — acoustic lo-fi with a folk soul.

C4C (Jesse Aymon Dettwiler) is a Swiss composer and producer, known for creating Folk-Hop — a warm blend of acoustic folk textures and lo-fi hip-hop rhythms.

Often described as: “If Bob Dylan made chill beats.”

Over the years, C4C’s music has been released and featured through independent scenes and labels across lo-fi / chillhop culture — while keeping a strong “human-made, organic” signature.

Story

C4C’s sound is shaped by mountains, lakes, and the kind of silence that makes you notice detail. You’ll hear finger-picked guitars, subtle textures, and drums that leave room to breathe.

The goal is simple: instrumentals that feel like fresh air — for studying, creating, walking, or slowing down.

Folk-Hop Radio

Beyond producing, C4C curates and develops Folk-Hop Radio — a network of playlists showcasing Folk-Hop and adjacent organic lo-fi sounds across platforms.
