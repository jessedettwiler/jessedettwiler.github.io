Bio

Swiss composer/producer — acoustic lo-fi with a folk soul.

C4C (Jesse Aymon Dettwiler) is a Swiss composer and producer crafting warm instrumental music where acoustic folk textures meet lo-fi hip-hop rhythms.

In 2020, he coined Folk-Hop — a blend of organic guitars, indie folk ambience, and calm drum grooves, built for focus, nature, and quiet moments.

Story

Raised between lakes and mountains, C4C’s music is shaped by outdoor quietness: soft guitars, organic textures, and drum patterns that breathe.

The goal is simple: instrumentals that feel like fresh air — for studying, creating, walking, or slowing down.
