Folk-Hop

Folk-Hop is a gentle meeting between two worlds: the folk culture of artists like Bob Dylan, and the lo-fi hip-hop legacy of J. Dilla.

Warm, human instrumentals built with acoustic guitars, ukuleles, flutes, banjos, harmonicas, kalimbas, mandolins — plus quiet “la-la-la” touches that feel close and real.

Bio

C4C is the artistic identity of Jesse Aymon Dettwiler. The name appeared in 2017 during early Folk-Hop experiments — it simply felt right, and never left.

Based in French-speaking Switzerland, between lakes and mountains, Jesse grew up in France with an Australian mother and a Swiss father.
