Folk-Hop

Folk-Hop is a gentle meeting between folk culture (Bob Dylan) and lo-fi hip-hop beat legacy (J. Dilla).

Warm, human instrumentals built with acoustic strings, folk instruments, and organic hip-hop drums.

Bio

C4C is the artistic identity of Jesse Aymon Dettwiler. The name appeared in 2017 during early Folk-Hop experiments.

Based in French-speaking Switzerland, between lakes and mountains. Music made to calm the mind and feel like fresh air.
